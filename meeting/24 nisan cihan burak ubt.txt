Influencer Value Proposition & Monetization

  - A formal playbook is required to clearly define the value for influencers.
  - Monetization Model:
      - Revenue Share: A 5-year share (e.g., 10%) of subscription revenue from referred businesses and premium users.
      - Paid Posts: A separate budget for paid posts, which influencers value more than temporary Stories.
  - Incentives for Smaller Influencers (<20k followers):
      - "Welcome Packages": Physical or digital gifts (e.g., branded merch, software trials) to secure Stories without cash.
      - Rationale: This builds relationships for future paid collaborations.
