Topics

Project Vision & Naming

  - Name: "Cortex" was selected over "Turkish Atlas" to create a universal, scalable brand.
      - Rationale: A neutral name is essential for attracting investors and expanding the framework to other diaspora communities (e.g., Indian, Filipino).
  - Parent Company: Cortex will operate under Qualtron Sinclair (QS), a growth partner firm with a portfolio of startups and networks.
      - Benefit: This affiliation provides immediate credibility and access to a pre-existing network of alumni and business groups.

Development Strategy

  - AI-Driven Documentation: An AI-driven process will be used to build a comprehensive MVP document, ensuring efficiency and a complete technical foundation.
      - Process: Prompt the AI to ask 50+ questions → Answer questions to define scope → AI generates an end-to-end MVP document (incl. backend, database, auth).
  - UI/UX: The design will avoid generic, AI-generated aesthetics by using an "organic" style with human elements and Turkish motifs, creating a distinct and authentic feel.

Core Features & Monetization

  - Consultants:
      - Profile: A professional page for experts (e.g., doctors, lawyers).
      - AI Twin: An AI chatbot or clone to answer questions 24/7.
      - Event Management: A tool for consultants to host and promote paid events.
  - Organizations:
      - Groups: Associations, NGOs, and schools.
      - Consulates: Official government entities.
      - Services: Membership management, donation processing, and event promotion.
  - Businesses:
      - Listings: A directory for all Turkish-owned businesses.
      - Job Board: A dedicated portal for job listings, a key traffic driver.
          - Monetization: Job posting is a premium feature, requiring a higher-tier subscription.
      - Discount Coupons: Businesses can sell coupons via the platform, with Cortex taking a revenue share.
      - Franchise/Partnership: A section for businesses to announce expansion or investment opportunities.
  - WhatsApp/Telegram Groups:
      - Directory: A central hub for discovering and joining diaspora chat groups.
      - Monetization: A WhatsApp bot will push sponsored content (ads, events) into approved groups.
          - Revenue Share: Group members who are also Cortex users will earn a share of ad revenue in their in-app wallets, creating a strong incentive to join the platform.
  - Events:
      - A central calendar for all diaspora events.
      - Monetization: A "Featured Event" option allows users to pay for prominent placement.
  - Map:
      - A map view to locate businesses and organizations.
      - Monetization: Businesses can pay to have their pins highlighted.

Traction & Growth Strategy

  - Initial User Acquisition:
      - Mass Migration: Target existing diaspora business councils (e.g., Dubai's Turkish Business Council, with 2,000+ businesses) for bulk onboarding.
      - Influencers: Partner with diaspora influencers to drive individual user adoption.
  - User Engagement:
      - Incentives: Use discount coupons and the WhatsApp revenue-share model to encourage sign-ups and activity.
      - Community: Leverage existing networks like Ottogether (the ODTÜ alumni atlas) as a model for growth.
